About Us

Birthed in 2010, the Pay4Me Mall is a virtual mall where store owners can set up their virtual stores FREE and individuals can shop at these stores through our platform; anytime, anywhere, anyplace.

A secure, convenient and comfortable online shopping environment, the Pay4Me Mall was set up for Nigerians, to enhance our internet culture and promote convenient shopping via a SECURE platform. See www.pay4me.com.

OUR VISION is to engineer a revolution in Online Shopping in Nigeria.

OUR MISSION is to promote convenience and flexibility in the day-to-day shopping activities of The Nigerian while promoting a secure online payment system that is Verisign Secured. See www.pay4me.com.

The Pay4Me Mall is located in Lekki, Lagos, Nigeria.
