How to Pay

We offer you diverse payment options to further ensure your shopping convenience. Payments can be made using any of the following:

  1. Payment by Ewallet
    • Register for an e-wallet account at www.pay4me.com.
    • Go to any Sterling Bank branch.
    • Put as much money on your e-Wallet as you would like.
    • Use the funds to pay online at any site where Pay4Me is accepted.

  2. Payment at Bank
    Enjoy the convenience of paying through any of the 2000+ branches of the 8 Banks currently on our platform. Click on our Pay@Bank and follow the instructions.

  3. VISA or Vpay
    Pay using your international VISA card or using our very own V-Pay, accepted only in Nigeria.
